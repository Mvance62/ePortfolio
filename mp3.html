Mark Vance's MP3: MP3 Digital Auto Project

For my MP3 I used Audacity to record my voice narration. Once I had my voice recorded I used compressor for leveling out the volume. The settings I used were: Threshold -12dB Noise floor -80dB Ratio 6:1 Attack time .5 Release time 1.0 I also used the envelop tool. With the envelope tool I created an amplitude envelop to track to adjust the volume. I also used the selection tool to to cut and delete sections of my voice audio and music. The music that I used I downloaded from iTunes. The songs are as follows: Wham Bam Shang-a-lang, George Philipp Telemann Symphony, Gerog Friedrich Handel Symphony. I know there is almost 9 minuets of music and my voice but I intend to edit down after I get more added to my eportfolio.
