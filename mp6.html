Mark Vance's MP6 Video Project

This video was created using Movavi
For my MP6 video I used the video editor Movavi to edit my video. I have used this before so it was much easier for me that using Imovie. I like using images on pages much better that the audio only in MP3. My background is in graphic design and I am an art teacher so images are important to me. I used many different transitions and titlles plus zooming in and out of my photos. I hope you like what I did and it gives you a better idea of who I am and my passion for teaching.
