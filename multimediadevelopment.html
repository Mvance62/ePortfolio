Screencastify
Screencastify has helped me to develop the necessary skills to be able to create quality videos to start flipping my classroom. This technology makes it easy to record any content displayed on your computer screen. Without this type of media tool it would be difficult to provide students with recorded instructional content. I use this in many ways from explaining the proper use of a camera, critiquing famous works of art or introducing famous artists. This allows me to prerecord information so that students can use their Chromebooks to view missed information or to help them clarify ideas or techniques. The “screencastify” link is an example of how I use this with my students.

Padlet
One way I use new technology in my classroom is with Padlet. This is an online application that allows my students to share and collaborate with each other ideas and artwork. They are able to get prompts from me and work independently on different projects. The technology frees me up to work one on one with students and to quickly assess the work they are doing. The students get the opportunity to share their ideas and work not only with me but with classmates as well. The link is an overview of a Padlet Wall I created for my 5th graders.
