 		 Membership to Deep Space Members Club
Deep Space Sparkle is an online art teacher’s resource that provides opportunities for art teachers to collaborate with other art teachers from around the country and around the world. Membership in the Sparklers Club opens the door to an exclusive Facebook group just for art teachers. The members of the Sparkler club have access to thousands of lesson plans, curriculum help, technical training classes and pod casts specific to teaching art to children. There is also an exclusive Facebook group for all members. This is a resource I use daily to help improve my lessons, assessments and creativity.
