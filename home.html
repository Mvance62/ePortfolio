Mark Vance's Technology Portfolio

Hi my name is Mark Vance and I am an elementary art teacher in the Brunswick City School District. I grew up in South Euclid and went to Brush High School. Not only am I a teacher but I have been a firefighter for the Brunswick Hills Fire Department for the past 17 years. I teach about 450 students from Kindergarten to 5th grade. Teaching is my second career. I ran my own painting business for 15 years before getting into teaching. I did my undergraduate at Bowling Green State University in the late 1980's and received my teaching licensure from Cleveland State University in 2008.

The purpose of this site is to demonstrate the skills I have developed through my coursework in the Master’s Program of Instructional technology at the University of Akron. The artifacts on this site are aligned with the National Educational Technology Standards and will show how I have integrated these skills into my teaching philosophy. Thank you for taking the time to look through this website. If you have any questions feel free to contact me using the "contact me" link below.
