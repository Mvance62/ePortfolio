 Class Dojo
Class Dojo is a great website most of the teachers in my building use for parent communication and behavior tracking. The students can earn points for positive behavior and can lose points for misbehaving. The site tracks each students points and each teacher can set up specific rewards. One of the main benefits is a "Class Story" for announcements and the Message feature to chat with a parent directly. Many parents download this application to their cell phone and can communicate instanteously. It is a great site to connect classrooms directly with parents in real time.

 Flipping my Classroom
To better serve my students I am starting to "flip" my classsroom. I am in the process of recording my lessons with my Iphone, Ipad or IPEVO presenter camera. This will enable students to view lessons at home, when they have been absent or if they do not understand something. These videos will be on a website I am creating that all my students will have access to with their Chromebooks. This is one way I am trying to reach the 21st century learner.
Design Document
This document is for a website I am designing that will hold classroom instructional videos for my students to view. This is a project several art teachers in my district are working on together. We are all "flipping" our classrooms to better serve our students. We will be sharing videos on lessons and techniques we teach. This will better serve our students and help align our curriculums.
